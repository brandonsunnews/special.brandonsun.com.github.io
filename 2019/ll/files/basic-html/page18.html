             GRAND VALLEY PARK & CAMPGROUND
               Located minutes from Brandon and just off the Trans-Can-
             ada Highway. Grand Valley is a nicely treed campground set in
             the beautiful Assiniboine River valley and is home to the historic
             Stott Site. The park is host to a lookout tower and interpretive
             trail that explains the rich First Nations history of the land.
               • Location: Off Grand Valley Road
               • Phone: 204-573-3798
